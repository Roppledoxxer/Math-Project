Circle Formulas

  • Diameter = 2 × Radius
  • Radius = Diameter ÷ 2
  • Circumference = 2 × π × r
  • Circumference = π × d
  • Area = π × r²
  • Use π = 3.14

Vocab

  • Area: the space inside a circle
  • Circumference: the distance around a circle
  • Radius: the distance from the center to the edge
  • Diameter: the distance across the circle through the center
  • Center: the middle of the circle

How to Calculate Circles

  • Find Area: Multiply Pi (3.1415) by the radius squared.
  • Find Circumference: Multiply the diameter by Pi.
  • Find Radius: Divide the diameter by two, or measure from the center to the edge.
  • Find Diameter: Multiply the radius by two, or measure straight across the center.
  • Locate Center: Find the exact point that is equidistant from all edges.
